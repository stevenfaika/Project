What is Lemme Help

Helping hands is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving those most at-risk to COVID-19: senior citizens, the immune compromised, and people with pre-exisiting medical condition whichput them at additional risk. our community volunteers and delivery partners deliver grocerises, medicine. and other necessary supplies to empower everyone to stay healthy and be a responisble as they can be.

How we Help

Helping Hands Community connects who must shelter in place with our volunteers and delivery partners so grocerises, medication and essential supplies get delivered safely. our technology platform make community volunteerism easy and reliable while providing an intuitive solution for partners to scale thier own impact.

our partner solutions allow organization to fulfill custom and batch requests at scale through the Helping Hand Community platform, while we connect individuals who neeed help and can offer through our website.
